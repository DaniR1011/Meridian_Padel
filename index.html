Frequently asked questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about our experiences, packages and logistics.

Where is Meridian Padel located?

Meridian Padel is based in Doha, Qatar. It was established in January 2026.

What does Meridian Padel offer?

Meridian Padel creates high-level sports experiences, characterised by offering premium services in different cities across Spain. Our offer includes agreements with the best padel academies, accommodation in luxury hotels, chauffeur services and gastronomic experiences in high-end restaurants.

Which cities does Meridian Padel offer?

Meridian Padel launches services in the main cities of interest in Spain: Madrid, Barcelona, Mallorca, Marbella, Alicante and Bilbao. However, we are expanding rapidly and working to offer more destinations.

How can I make a booking with Meridian Padel?

Leave us your details and tell us which services you are interested on, and we will contact you.

What can I do if I want to go to one of the destinations but for a different number of days than the packages offered?

Meridian Padel offers the possibility of purchasing pre-designed packages and also provides a fully personalised service tailored to the needs of each customer.
